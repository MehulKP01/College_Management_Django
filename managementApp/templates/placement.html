Placement Cell

The placement cell actively collaborates with companies to organize recruitment drives, job fairs, and campus interviews. These events provide a platform for students to interact with recruiters, showcase their skills, and explore job opportunities. The placement cell also assists in coordinating internships and industry visits, allowing students to gain practical exposure to their chosen fields.

Our Recruiters

Recruiters are adept at conducting interviews and assessing candidates' skills, qualifications, and cultural fit within the company. They play a crucial role in building and maintaining a strong talent pipeline, ensuring a continuous influx of qualified individuals. Additionally, recruiters stay informed about industry trends, salary benchmarks, and employment laws to make informed decisions and provide valuable insights to both candidates and hiring managers.
